Astronaut painting [untitled]

1969

Acrylic painting of an astronaut on the moon,

Provenance

Cleveland, OH (I think) or Milford, CT

Notes

According to my mother, Sheila Reuven, my father was unsurprisingly completely obsessed with the Apollo 11 moon landing and watched it non-stop. 

Based on the photo of Buzz Aldrin taken by Neil Armstrong: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Aldrin_Apollo_11.jpg
